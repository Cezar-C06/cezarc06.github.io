    The DCS F-14

    Welcome! Whether you're a seasoned DCS player with other aircraft, or new to the game altogether, if you're looking to learn the basics of fighting in the F-14, you're at the right place!

    Firstly, the F-14 is old. Really old. Designed in the 60s, it entered service in the mid 70s. This is a contrast to other aircraft you may the familiar with. The F-18, F-16, and F-15 all were designed in the 70s, and entered service in the 80s, making the F-14 nearly a decade older, in an age of rapid aviation development. The consequences of this are very visible: No FlyByWire system, A HUD (heads up display) that is more for decoration than any actual use, and of course the flight characteristics of a brick with wings.

    However, don't be discouraged! The F-14 may be an old girl, but this old girl still has some lethal teeth, and can put up quite the fight even against modern AMRAAM flinging aircraft
